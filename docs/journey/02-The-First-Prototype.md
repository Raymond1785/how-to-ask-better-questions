# Chapter 2 — The First Prototype

For weeks,

everything had existed only in my head.

Every screen.

Every workflow.

Every idea.

They all seemed to fit together.

At least,

that's what I thought.

I had been building.

One piece at a time.

Trying.

Changing.

Trying again.

Then,

for the first time,

I could actually see it.

It wasn't bad.

But it wasn't what I had imagined.

Some pages felt empty.

Others were trying to say too much.

Some parts looked nice.

But they didn't say much at all.

So I started changing things.

Move this.

Delete that.

Rewrite a sentence.

Replace a picture.

Look again.

Every version looked better.

But it still didn't feel right.

I couldn't explain why.

Something just felt...

off.

I kept looking at it.

Changing it.

Looking again.

And that feeling

stayed with me.
