## 9. The Product Isn’t the Hero

We often become attached to the products we build.

We know how much time, thought, and effort went into them.

But to the user, a product is simply a tool.

Like a boat, its job is to carry someone across the river.

Once they reach the other side, the boat is still a boat.

What matters is that they got where they needed to go.

The same is true for software.

**Its value comes from whether it helps someone understand something more easily than before.**

The product does not need to be the hero.

**It just needs to get people to the other side.**
