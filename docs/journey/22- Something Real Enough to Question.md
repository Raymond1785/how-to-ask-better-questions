## 22. Something Real Enough to Question

When I first started building prototypes, I wanted them to look good.

I wanted the idea to feel convincing.

But the first prototype has a different job.

An idea in your head can feel perfect.

Until you see it.

Then the questions begin.

What is missing?

Does this actually help?

Is this even the right problem?

The first prototype isn't built to impress anyone.

It is built to give you something real enough to question.
