## 5. Design an Experience, Not a Presentation

While building my side project, I realized it wasn't enough to visualize data.

It had to help people understand it.

That changed how I designed every function.

Instead of asking,

> "Does this look better?"

I started asking,

> "Is this easier to understand?"

Repeated testing and validation became part of the design process.

Not to make it look better.

But to make it feel more natural.

That was the difference between showing information and creating an experience.
