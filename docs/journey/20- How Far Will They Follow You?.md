## 20. How Far Will They Follow You?

After thinking about a demo as a journey, another thought came to me.

We spend a lot of time thinking about what we want to show.

But while we are presenting, the audience is making decisions too.

Does this person really understand the problem?

Is this worth my time?

A demo is not only about showing the audience your product.

The audience is also deciding how far they are willing to follow you.

You may have planned the entire journey.

But that does not mean they will stay until the end.

Like a good magic trick, attention is everything.

Every claim can build trust or weaken it.

Every explanation can create interest or lose it.

Every step gives the audience another reason to continue — or to leave.

That changed the way I think about presenting.

The question is not only:

**What do I want to show next?**

It is also:

**Have I given them a reason to follow me there?**
